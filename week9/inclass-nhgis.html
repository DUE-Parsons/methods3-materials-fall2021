In-class Exercises

Download and process data from NHGIS and explore with Plotly

  1. Go to nhgis and download race/ethnicity data for every census tract in the country using NHGIS.
  2. Select Geographic Levels = census tract, Years = 2020, Data = P2. Hispanic or Latino, and Not Hispanic or Latino by Race.
  3. Sign up for a free account with NHGIS and download the data.
  4. Open the codebook and the data.
  5. Save a version of the data as a csv in your processed folder and select the id variables you need to join to your shapefile and all of the Population of one race alone variables and create the following new variables:
    • Percent White alone, Not Hispanic
    • Percent Hispanic or Latino
    • Percent Black or African Alone, Not Hispanic
    • Percent Asian Alone, Not Hispanic
  6. Add csv to QGIS and join to your statewide affordability shapefile.
  7. Create a new plotly scatterplot to compare affordability to your race/ethnicity variables.
  8. For your homework assignment, you will continue to work with this data.
