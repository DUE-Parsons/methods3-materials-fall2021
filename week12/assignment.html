Assignment 12

Use R to prepare data for your final project

Write a script in R to import data and process it to use in QGIS for your final project or another project.

This could be:

  • Geocoding a dataset: preparing the data in R and geocoding in QGIS
  • Processinging census data in R to join to a shapefile in QGIS:
    • read_csv() to import data
    • select() the columns
    • rename() the columns
    • mutate() to create new columns (ex: pct_latinx)
    • write_csv() to export to your computer and import in QGIS

Submitting your work

Submit your R script, any data files you use in the script, and a short description of what you accomplished including a description or screenshot of what you accomplished in QGIS.
