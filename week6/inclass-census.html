Join census data to calculate covid rates

  1. Go the the census website and search for population data.
  2. Select the first entry - Race
  3. Select Customize Table, then Download
  4. Open the data in Excel or similar and explore the data, remove one of the header columns, and identify the population field.
  5. In QGIS, reopen the covid map project from the last in-class exercise.
  6. Join the census spreadsheet to the state shapefile with covid data already joined.
  7. Calculate a new field of covid cases or deaths per 100,000 people.
  8. Make a choropleth showing your new covid statistic.
  9. Export your state dataset to the week6/output folder.
